Data Protection & Compliance

The standard your industry demands, answered.

✓

UK data residency

AI analysis pinned to Google Cloud London (europe-west2); data stored in Supabase London (eu-west-2). Nothing sensitive leaves the UK.

✓

DPAs on every processor

Google Cloud and Supabase both operate under signed Data Processing Agreements. Replit hosts only the interface and stores no client data.

✓

No training on your data

Runs on Gemini Enterprise (Vertex AI), where Google contractually commits inputs and outputs are never used to train its models.

✓

Locked-down access

Google login with two-factor authentication, row-level security, and a built-in audit log of who accessed what.
